About Me

My name is Ajay Horne and I am a 12 year old boy from Durham, NC. My dream is to be a professional football player. I learned to catch when I was 5 and from then on I've been training my whole life! I train almost everyday, catching and throwing, running routes, and working on my fitness.

Unfortunately, my school doesn't have a football team in the middle or highschool. So I've played i9 flag football leauges just so I can have some game experience. My dad coaches and I play. As of before the 2024 season I've won 2 championships. We're going for another one this year! I also may want to go to North Carolina School of Science and Math for my junior and senior years of high school because they have a football team there. Another option I have is a tackle football leauge in my area that my coach recently told me about.

In my games I normally play center, quarterback or wide reciever. I can throw and move in the pocket well, and my snap is pretty good, but my strong suit is being lined up as a wide reciever. I'm built like one, lean but fast. It is my normal position and I've learned a lot from my experience. My dad is my best coach on and off the football field. When we are not at practice, we practice one on one. He's taught me so much strategy and route running skills to use to make my routes crisp and clean.
